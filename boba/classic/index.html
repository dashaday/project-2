Classic Milk Tea

If you are new to drinking boba or would just prefer a simple flavor, try out our Classic Milk Tea! Our Classic Milk Tea consists of freshly brewed black tea, half a cup of milk (your choice of whole, skim, or almond), sugar, tapioca pearls, and ice cubes to keep your drink icy cool. This drink is silky smooth--perfect for those who are looking for a milk tea staple.
